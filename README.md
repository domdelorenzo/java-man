### ***Getting Started***

1. Navigate to [https://javaman.surge.sh/](https://javaman.surge.sh/)
2. Click *Insert Coin* to begin.
3. Move your player with arrow keys on your keyboard
4. "Eat" all of the dots to win
5. Avoid the ghosts!
